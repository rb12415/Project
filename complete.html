This game was built by using Unity and is coded in C#. The game is not long, but it is a fun small project to get me familiar with C#. Basically the game is about a regular treasure hunter trying to find a certain amount of drugs to stay warm during the winter. Once you complete the first stage you'll proceed to the second stage where you are in the desert. The desert will have rings that you must collect so you can complete the game.
